About

Cilvi, or Creative Instructions for Limited VIsion, is a website created to format instructions for creative projects, specifically crochet and knitting.
Crafters who have trouble reading traditional fiber art patterns (limited vision, reading disabilities) can use the application for patterns to make for an accessible pattern reading experience.
Pattern designers can also utilize the application, uploading pre-existing patterns or using our simple interface to make creating an alternate accessible pattern that much easier!
